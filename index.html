About Me:

Hello! I'm Zoe Collins. I've been programming since a summer camp when I was 12 that introduced me to Scratch, and from there kept digging into everything I could get my hands on. My goal in life is to show the world what I've loved doing since I was a kid, and show loud and proud just what women in STEM can accomplish!

I began my formal programming education at Cleveland State University, later moving on to The Ohio State University, pursuing Computer Science at both of those universities. With already having self-taught interests in programming, college just never seemed like a good fit for me. I was later introduced to The Ohio State Unviersity coding bootcamp which seemed much more my style of hands-on apply what you know education. That's lead me to where I am today!

I've worked in many languages, including: Java, C#, Lua, HTML/CSS, JavaScript, and SQL, with almost all of those having been applied to a software developer co-op through Emerson Electric. I am confident that I can make my mark wherever I may end up, and hope that my projects below can give a little taste of what I'm capable of.
